About the Project

This project aims to develop an eco-friendly village using scientific principles from Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Computer Science.

Biology: Sustainable Agriculture

Our biology team has focused on creating an aeroponic and hydroponic farming system. These systems require no soil and use significantly less water than traditional farming, making them perfect for an eco-friendly village.

Chemistry: Water Purification

The chemistry team has developed a system that purifies and recycles wastewater. This system uses advanced filtration and chemical processes to ensure that water remains clean and safe for consumption.

Physics: Renewable Energy & Hydraulic Systems

Physics plays a crucial role in our project with a focus on renewable energy sources such as solar and hydroelectric power. Our team has also designed hydraulic jack bridges, which are energy-efficient and sustainable for infrastructure.

Computer Science: Smart Village Systems

Our computer science team is responsible for developing the smart village design, which includes a website and an interactive platform for residents. The system will monitor energy consumption, water usage, and farming efficiency in real-time.
