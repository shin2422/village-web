About NVL International Academy

We are dedicated to providing quality education while fostering innovation and sustainability. Our team, Pre-IGCSE Section 7, has worked together on this Eco-Friendly Village project, highlighting the importance of sustainability through science and technology.

Class: Pre-IGCSE Section 7

Biology: Aeroponics & Hydroponics

Our biology team has developed models of aeroponics and hydroponics, two soil-less farming techniques that help conserve water while growing plants. These techniques are integral to our Eco-Friendly Village's sustainability.

  • Aeroponics: Growing plants using mist without soil.
  • Hydroponics: Growing plants in nutrient-rich water without soil.

Chemistry: Water Purification

Our chemistry team has developed a water purification model using natural materials such as sand, charcoal, and cotton. This model simulates how a natural filtration system can clean contaminated water for safe use.

Physics: Hydraulic Jack Bridge

Our physics team has demonstrated hydraulic power using a hydraulic jack bridge. This project uses syringes and pumps to lift the bridge, showing how fluid mechanics can support heavy loads.

Computer Science: Smart Village Technology

We have developed a smart village website that integrates renewable energy, water management, and agriculture into a single interface. This project uses various technologies such as JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Node.js to create an interactive experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is aeroponics?

A: Aeroponics is a method of growing plants in air or mist without using soil.

Q: How does the hydraulic jack bridge work?

A: The hydraulic jack bridge uses a syringe to lift the bridge, simulating hydraulic force.
